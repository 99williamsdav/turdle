How to play

  • Wordle with a turbo twist — race to solve fastest!
  • Score points by solving quickly and in as few guesses as possible.
  • Invalid words cost you points, so choose wisely.
  • GREEN letters are in the right spot.
  • YELLOW letters are somewhere else in the word.
  • GREY letters aren’t in the puzzle at all.
  • Type your guesses or tap them on the virtual keyboard.
  • The keyboard colours show the clues you’ve gathered so far.
  • If you’re stuck, spend points at the bottom to buy a clue.
